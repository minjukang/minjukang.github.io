Introduction

Mobile Robotics & Intelligence Laboratory (MORIN Lab) is a research group focused on intelligent control and autonomy of vehicles and mobile robotic systems. We are conducting research ondynamics, guidance, control and estimation across the full range of manned and unmanned vehicle applications . We are also working on developing innovative algorithms and techniques which are applicable to all types of vehicles and mobile robotic systems.


Prospective Students and Postdoc Researchers

We are looking for qualified and highly motivated students and postdocs with various engineering and science backgrounds (e.g., mechanical & aerospace engineering, ocean engineering, electrical engineering, computer science, etc.) to join our group.
